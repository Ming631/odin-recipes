Home Made Cotton Candy!

Soft yet sticky delicious cotton candy

Hardly anything brings us back to our childhoods more than cotton candy. There’s just something awe-inspiring about this seemingly physics-defying candy made from sugar and air.
Plus, it has such a distinct flavor, cotton candy worked its way into a whole host of other treats and drinks, from ice cream to cocktails.


Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Corn syrup
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Raspberry, lemon, almond, vanilla, or other extract or oil for flavoring
  • Food coloring
  • Candy thermometer
  • Parchment paper
  • Side note: make sure you have an item to whip/spin your cotton candy

Directions

  1. Mix the sugar, corn syrup, water, and salt in a large heavy-bottomed pot, then stir over medium-high heat until all the sugar is melted
  2. Transfer sugar mixture into a large heat-safe bowl to avoid burning sugar
  3. Dip the tines of your whisk into the mixture, then quickly drizzle the thinning sugar strands back and forth across the parchment paper until you have a sizeable “nest” of candy strands
  4. Serve and enjoy!
